About Us

At Kimo's Computer Clinic, we combine technical expertise with a care-first mindset. Based in San Mateo, San Pablo City, Laguna, we provide professional computer repair, preventative maintenance, and quality replacement parts — treating every machine like a patient that deserves attentive care.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Provide reliable, affordable, and health-focused computer repair services that extend the life of devices and reduce electronic waste.

Vision: To be the most trusted "clinic" for PCs in San Pablo City, known for expert repairs, honest advice, and genuine care for customers' devices.
